Learn & Grow

We are here to make your digital classroom experience as seamless as possible. Our tools make it simple to manage and submit your assignments, view and share study material and resources, keep a track of your pending tasks and help you make most of your remote learning experience

Submitting Assignments

Teachers can easily upload assignments, set deadlines and assign them to students. The students too can view & submit these assignments and add comments if necessary.

Sharing Study Material

Not only can the teachers upload assignments, but can also share study material and resources so that the students can better understand the matter.

To Do List

Digital Board offers students a to-do list so that they can easily keep a track of what all tasks have been done, and what all tasks have been assigned.

Pin Important Resources

Teachers can pin important resources at the very start, so that the students don't find it difficult to navigate to them at the time of need.

Login via Google

By providing easy login and logout capability via Social Accounts, we have removed the hassle of remembering another set of login credentials.

Mobile Friendly View

No desktop?
No worries! We got you covered. Although developed as a desktop-first application, Digital Board is fully responsive and will work on screens of all sizes.
